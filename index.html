About

Manx helps busy service professionals save time and money by mapping out the best possible route for each of their stops. Gone are the days where you will have to look at a map each morning and call clients last minute because their location is just too far.

Whether you're an electrician looking to plan out your house calls for the week, a cleaning company looking to split up your team and efficiently cover territories, or a consultant looking for the best way to schedule clients, Manx is the one tool you need to get back more time in your day!
